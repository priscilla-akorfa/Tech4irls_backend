Integer: refers to a whole number for instance 4

Float: refers to numbers that contains decimal points or they are simply decimal numbers eg. 4.40

String: refers to a sequence of characters in a quote for instance "Hello World"

List: refers to a bunch of sequential data or an ordered collection of items. eg. list1=[1, 2, 3, 4]

Tuple: refers to refers to an ordered collection of items which are similar to lists but instead of a square brackets, they are in a parenthesis. Additionally, they cannot be modified. eg. (1, 2, 3, 4)

Set: refers to values that are unodered and they are notified by curly brackets. {1, 2, 3, 4}
